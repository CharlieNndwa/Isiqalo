About Us

Our story began with a simple dream: to nurture the potential in every child, regardless of background. We believe every kid deserves a quality education, filled with wonder and discovery. So we built our school on the pillars of love, respect, and academic excellence. We handpick our teachers, not just for their knowledge, but for their kind hearts and passion for learning. They become mentors, friends, and cheerleaders, guiding each child on their unique journey.
Nestled in the heart of Vereeniging, at 55th Beaconsfield Avenue, you'll find Isiqalo Primary School. It's not just a school, it's a vibrant village where young bright minds can blossom and prosper. Imagine a place where laughter echoes through classrooms, where curiosity sparks in curious eyes, and where every child feels safe and cherished. That's Isiqalo for you! 🙂

Principal's Message

Dear Parents and all Well-Wishers,

It is with immense pride and excitement that we open our doors to the vibrant communities within the Vaal! Located in Vereeniging, our school stands as a beacon of hope, a place where young minds blossom and dreams take flight.

We are not just bricks and mortar; we are a family, a nurturing ground where curiosity ignites, and potential explodes. We believe in every child's unique spark, and our curriculum is designed to fan those flames into roaring infernos of creativity, critical thinking, and compassion.

At Isiqalo Primary School, your child will embark on a transformative journey, one that goes beyond rote learning and text-heavy textbooks. We offer a kaleidoscope of experiences, where academic rigor dances with hands-on exploration, and technology seamlessly blends with traditional values

So, dear parents, if you seek a school that celebrates individuality, nurtures curiosity, and fosters a love for learning, your search ends here. At Isiqalo Primary, we welcome applications with open arms (and even wider hearts!). Come, join us as we build a community of bright minds, compassionate souls, and beacons of tomorrow.

Let the learning adventure begin & Remember, Knowledge is Power!


Head Principal
