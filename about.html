About Us

"Education is the passport to the universe; curiosity is the fuel."

Our school has a rich history of nurturing young minds and fostering a passion for learning in the Vaal area. We are guided by a vision to create a vibrant and inclusive learning environment where every student feels empowered to reach their full potential. Our mission is to provide a quality education that challenges and supports students to become lifelong learners, critical thinkers, and responsible citizens who are prepared to make a positive impact on the world.

Our History

Our journey started in 2018 upon registering the school, we managed to obtain the approval from GDE in 2023, now in 2024 we are welcoming students to be a part of our history

Our Vision

Isiqalo Primary School envisions a future where every learner scales academic heights. We stand resolute in our pursuit of a 100% pass rate, laying the firm foundation for success while fostering a love for learning.

Our Mission

To empower every child to reach their full potential, nurturing a love for learning within a supportive community. Through unwavering dedication and a 100% pass rate goal, we guide them to confidently embrace the future, ready to shine.
